Earning Money by Helping Your Community, What’s Not to Love?

You can join a worldwide movement to preserve the environment by having fun competing with other users across the world who are passionate about helping their community.

Our web application helps pinpoint heavily polluted areas of your local city so that you can locate and help clean up the trash. By providing us with before and after photos of the area you helped clean up, you can earn points to rank on our local and global leaderboards. Cash prizes are given to top monthly scorers.

Clean Up the Town with the Largest Resource Available: The People

With every passing day, Metropolitan cities like Los Angeles, Sacramento, New York, and Chicago are becoming increasingly populated and polluted.

The best way to clean up is to tap into every city’s greatest resource: their people. Partnering with our app further incentivizes users to clean up their city and allows for more seamless updates through improved tools to better track and reduce pollution.
